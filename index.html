Video Hole: Episode I

Nothing to see here except a perfectly ordinary video store.

Video Hole is an ongoing, episodic adventure game series revolving around a series of strange completely normal events that transpire in your local, intergalactic video store. In episode one, it’s business as usual as you navigate your soul-crushing job while solving mysteries and meeting new characters. Of course, things are never quite what they seem in the darkly mysterious world of cross-temporal, hard-format film rental.


Awarded “Best Overall,” 2020 Indie Cluster Arcade
